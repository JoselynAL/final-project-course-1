Sources

  • Fintech in Ecuador refers to companies that use technological innovation to offer financial services in an efficient and disruptive manner. Ecuador has recently approved the Fintech Law, which aims to provide legal certainty to financial technology companies operating in the country.

  • The Telecommunications Law in Ecuador aims to promote the development and strengthening of the telecommunications sector. It establishes the powers of administration, regulation, control and management throughout the national territory.
